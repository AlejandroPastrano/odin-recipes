Greek Couscous Salad

This hearty Greek couscous salad uses Israeli couscous. So delicious!

This Greek Couscous Salad is refreshing, super flavorful and packed with tomatoes, cucumber, feta cheese and kalamata olives. Plus this delicious salad is ready in less than 20 minutes! Ideal to bring along to parties, for the buffet table or a make ahead dish for when you are hosting!
